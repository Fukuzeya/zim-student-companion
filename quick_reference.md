# ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is Zim Student Companion?
Zim Student Companion is an AI-powered study assistant that helps Zimbabwean students prepare for ZIMSEC exams. It works through WhatsApp - no app download needed.

### How is it different from other study apps?
- **WhatsApp-based** - No download required
- **ZIMSEC-specific** - Built for Zimbabwe's curriculum
- **AI that teaches** - Guides students, doesn't just give answers
- **Parent reports** - Weekly updates for parents
- **Local payments** - EcoCash, OneMoney accepted

### What grades/levels does it cover?
- Primary: Grade 1-7
- Secondary: Form 1-4 (O-Level)
- Advanced: Form 5-6 (A-Level)

### What subjects are available?
Mathematics, English, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Geography, History, Accounting, Commerce, Shona, Ndebele, and more. Full list in the app.

## For Students

### How do I start?
1. Save +263 XX XXX XXXX to your phone
2. Open WhatsApp and send "Hi"
3. Follow the setup prompts (takes 2 minutes)
4. Start practicing!

### Why doesn't it just give me the answer?
Our AI uses the "Socratic Method" - asking guiding questions that help you discover answers yourself. This builds real understanding that helps in exams, not just copying answers.

### What are XP points?
XP (Experience Points) are rewards for learning. Earn XP by answering correctly, maintaining streaks, and joining competitions. More XP = higher levels and badges!

### How do streaks work?
Practice at least once per day to build a streak. Longer streaks earn bonus rewards. Miss a day and your streak resets to zero.

### Can I use it for homework?
Yes! Type "ask" followed by your question, and the AI will help guide you to the answer.

### What if I'm stuck on a question?
Type "hint" to get a clue. You get 3 hints per question. Hints help point you in the right direction without giving away the answer.

## For Parents

### How do I monitor my child's progress?
1. Ask your child to type "parent code" in their chat
2. They'll receive a 6-digit code
3. Send this code to the same WhatsApp number
4. You'll be linked and receive weekly reports

### What information will I see?
- Questions attempted and accuracy
- Time spent studying
- Subjects practiced
- Strengths and areas needing work
- Streak status and achievements

### Can I see what questions my child is asking?
For privacy, you see summary statistics, not individual conversations. This encourages honest learning while keeping you informed.

### Will I be notified if my child isn't studying?
Yes, if you're linked, you'll receive alerts if your child hasn't practiced in 3+ days.

## Technical Questions

### Do I need to download an app?
No! It works entirely through WhatsApp. Just save our number and start chatting.

### Does it work on basic phones?
It works on any phone with WhatsApp. Some older phones may have limited features.

### How much data does it use?
Very little - about 1-2 MB per practice session. Much less than watching videos or browsing social media.

### Is my data safe?
Yes. We don't share personal data with third parties. All conversations are encrypted by WhatsApp. We follow data protection best practices.

### What if the bot doesn't respond?
1. Wait 30 seconds and try again
2. Send "menu" to reset
3. Check your internet connection
4. Contact support if it continues
